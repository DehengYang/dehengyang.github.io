I'm a researcher of software engineering. I have obtained both my Bachelor and PhD degree from the National University of Defense Technology, supervised by Prof. Xiaoguang Mao. My research interests include:

  • Automated Program Repair

  • Automated Fault Localization

  • Program Analysis for both Software and Hardware Programs
